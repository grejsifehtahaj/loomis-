About Loomis

Trusted hardware expertise for Leicester homes and trades.

Loomis is a family-run hardware store that has proudly served Leicester for over 20 years and has found its long-term home on the lively Narborough Road. Known for its extensive product range, fair pricing, and friendly, helpful customer service, Loomis has become a trusted destination for both the local community and customers who travel from further afield to shop there. The store stocks an impressive selection of tools, plumbing supplies, electrical components, paint, ironmongery, key cutting services, and everyday DIY essentials. Despite its compact size, customers often compare Loomis to an "Aladdin's cave" of hardware, thanks to the remarkable variety of products available-offering the choice and convenience often associated with much larger hardware stores. Whether it's a quick purchase, a home repair, or a larger DIY project, Loomis has dedicated and knowledgeable staff ready to help customers find exactly what they need.
